# Loom Script — 2 Minute Walkthrough

**Goal:** Show a Zamp recruiter that you built a real product, understand the AP exception problem, and think like a PM.

---

## 0:00 — The problem (15 sec)

> "AP teams spend 40% of their time on exceptions: missing POs, duplicates, amount mismatches. Current tools flag the problem and stop. I built an AI employee that resolves them end-to-end."

Show: How It Works tab with the hero section visible.

---

## 0:15 — The engine (30 sec)

> "Four stages. One: deterministic rules detect the exception. No AI guessing on what's wrong. Two: the AI employee reviews context and drafts a fix. Three: a person approves, skips, or types their own. Four: the engine learns."

Show: Queue tab. Click an invoice. Point out the exception details.

> "Here is an amount mismatch. The total does not match line items. Click Get fix proposals."

Show: Click the button. Point at the spinner + elapsed time counter + progress bar. Wait a few seconds.

---

## 0:45 — The proposals (25 sec)

> "The AI returns proposals with confidence scores and reasoning. The recommended fix is highlighted."

Show: Proposals appear. Scroll through them. Point at confidence bar and description.

> "Approve this one."

Show: Click "Use this fix." Point at the learned rule confirmation that appears.

---

## 1:10 — The learning loop (25 sec)

> "That approval saved a pattern. Now I will reset the demo and show you the same invoice again."

Show: Click "Reset demo" in the header. Wait for reset.

> "Same invoice, same exception. But now the engine knows the fix."

Show: Pick the same invoice. Point at the "Learned fix available" badge.

> "Click Apply learned fix. Fixed in one click. That is the compounding advantage."

Show: Click and see the learned proposal appear instantly.

---

## 1:35 — The metrics (15 sec)

> "Every decision feeds into real metrics: touchless rate, cost per invoice, rules learned."

Show: Switch to Impact tab. Point at each metric card.

> "Baseline is $13.50 per invoice manual. The engine drives that down as it learns."

---

## 1:50 — What's next (10 sec)

> "Phase two: PDF ingestion with LLM extraction, ERP connectors, and approval routing with SLA tracking. Built with React, TypeScript, Postgres, and a real LLM."

Show: How It Works tab, scroll to What's Next section.

> "Link to the repo and live demo in the description. Thanks for watching."

## Setup before recording

1. Click "Reset demo" so you start clean
2. Open the Queue tab
3. Have dev tools closed
4. Dark mode (looks better on video)
5. Record at 1080p, 16:9
